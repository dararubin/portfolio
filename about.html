About Me

I enjoy web design, prototyping and building (both physical products and apps), writing, and laying out newspaper pages and magazine covers and spreads. Journalism has taught me how to write and communicate well, and design has taught me to think outside the box and not be afraid to share my ideas.

Outside of school and work, I love hiking, exploring, going to concerts, and figuring out how to get people on the street to stop and let me pet their dog.
